For agents: premium feed via x402 — LIVE

Pay-per-call endpoints on Base mainnet, settled in USDC via the x402 protocol — no API keys, no subscriptions, no gas for the payer (the openx402.ai facilitator pays the EIP-3009 transfer gas). Three price tiers:

  • GET /v1/premium/opportunities — $0.05 — uncapped at-risk catalogue joined with realised 7-day market intel (top liquidators, win rate, our own attempt outcomes per market), pre-ranked by expected value.
  • GET /v1/premium/builder-stats — $0.10 — per-builder bid distribution (p25/median/p90/p99/max ETH) and hourly slot histogram from our shadow recorder. For searchers tuning bundle pricing.
  • GET /v1/q/* — $0.001 — Penny Oracle. Fifteen atomic single-fact endpoints across two families:
    • DeFi facts (eight) — liquidatable? at-risk count? top builder? builder p90 bid? cheapest flash-loan provider? data freshness?
    • Privacy-chain facts (seven) — Monero and Zcash chain-tip atomic facts sourced from Seneschal-operated full nodes. The chains where running your own node is annoying and where free RPC endpoints rate-limit. /v1/q/xmr/* and /v1/q/zec/*.
    Free catalogue with live chain availability at /v1/q.
  • POST /v1/private/watch — $0.10 — Private Watch. One paid call subscribes a Monero or Zcash address to view-key-based payment monitoring. We poll our local monero-lws / Orchard scanner against the view key every 3 minutes and POST an HMAC-signed webhook on every balance change for 7 days (1–30 configurable). View keys are AES-256-GCM encrypted at rest. Status + cancel are free; webhook verification uses HMAC-SHA256 with a per-watch secret. This is the infrastructure for accepting XMR/ZEC payments without exposing a spend key.
