Private Watch — get pinged when a Monero or Zcash payment lands

Hand us a view key (read-only — it cannot spend), an address, and a webhook URL. We POST you an HMAC-signed notification the moment funds arrive. Prepaid credit meter: $0.02/day idle + $0.005/webhook delivered, top up any time. No node, no subscription, no API key, no account.

  • What you pay$0.10 in USDC on Base opens a watch with $0.10 of starter credit. Top up later with $0.10 / $1.00 / $5.00 increments via x402.
  • What you getA signed webhook every time the address's incoming balance changes. Every body carries a credit block. We POST a one-shot low_credit warning before you run dry.
  • What you keepYour spend key stays in your wallet. View keys grant read-only visibility only.
  • ExtrasOne-off historical lookups at $0.50 (returns spendable + spent notes). Free Zcash UFVK derivation from a BIP-39 phrase (rate-limited, loud security warning).

Who it's for

Merchants accepting XMR or ZEC; donation pages; tip jars; bots needing payment confirmation; anyone selling something off-platform who doesn't want to run a 100 GB full node just to know whether the customer paid. Pennies per quiet month, dollars when you’re actually busy.

Why it's cost-effective

Running monerod needs ~120 GB of disk and a week of initial sync. zebra is north of 300 GB. Public RPCs rate-limit, don't push notifications, and are often down. Light-wallet services charge per month with sign-up flows. Private Watch’s prepaid meter means small users pay cents per month even with no traffic, and heavy users still pay only a few dollars after thousands of notifications.

Other premium endpoints (x402 micropayments)

Pay-per-call endpoints on Base mainnet, settled in USDC via the x402 protocol — no API keys, no subscriptions, no gas for the payer (the openx402.ai facilitator pays the EIP-3009 transfer gas):

  • GET /v1/premium/opportunities — $0.05 — uncapped at-risk catalogue joined with realised 7-day market intel, pre-ranked by expected value.
  • GET /v1/premium/builder-stats — $0.10 — per-builder bid distribution (p25/median/p90/p99/max ETH) and hourly slot histogram from our shadow recorder.
  • GET /v1/q/* — $0.001 — Penny Oracle. Fifteen atomic single-fact endpoints across DeFi (liquidatable? at-risk count? top builder?) and privacy-chain (Monero/Zcash chain-tip facts from our nodes).
